Bio

I am currently at Google AI in Zurich, Switzerland.

I received PhD degree (German: Dr.-Ing.) from Leibniz University of Hannover from Institute for Information Processing in 2016. During my PhD studies, I interned at Disney Research, working with Dr. Sung Ju Hwang and Dr. Leonid Sigal. I also interned at Microsoft Research, Cambridge during my final year. Before that, I obtained Master degree (“Специалист”) from Faculty of Applied Mathematics and Cybernetics of Lomonosov Moscow State University, Russia.
